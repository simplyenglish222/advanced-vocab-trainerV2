Project overview

What the project contains

  • Thirty thematic chapters covering major social, economic, scientific, cultural and environmental debates.
  • Advanced collocations, essential language, academic frameworks and reusable phrasal verbs with Russian support and pronunciation.
  • Original long-form readings, extended C2 idea-building essays and realistic IELTS Task 2 models.
  • Thirty developed speaking models per chapter, grammar transformations and reusable academic sentence patterns.
  • Persistent progress tracking, Quick Notes and responsive layouts designed for desktop and iPhone screens.
Recommended study sequence
  1. Open one topic chapter and build background knowledge.
  2. Study the new vocabulary and complete contextual retrieval.
  3. Analyse the reading and model essays.
  4. Record or write the speaking and writing answers.
  5. Use the master vocabulary and grammar courses for cumulative revision.
